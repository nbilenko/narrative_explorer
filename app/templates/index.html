NARRATIVE EXPLORER

Visual explorer of character co-occurrence and sentiment trajectory in written texts

About this visualization

Choose a text from the database or upload your own text file to create a new visualization.

The sentiment rating of each sentence in the story is shown on a scale from strongly negative to strongly positive. Mouse over each bar to read the sentence.

The co-occurrences of the characters in the story are displayed in a chord diagram. Click a character's name to highlight the sentences in which they appear. Drag the handles on the sentiment graph to choose a segment of the story.
